About the course

Information Analytics treats the new ways to derive meaning from information or (big) data. Information has become more diverse and fluid, and significance is often taken for granted in analysis or use of data. However, the analysis, use and re-use also creates new data, with diverse and often incommensurable meanings. This course explores the contexts of use, practical methods and theoretical implications of contemporary information analytics.
